# Pocoyo  
![Pocoyo Normal](https://github.com/Mido8686/Pocoyo-Encyclopedia/blob/main/Characters/Pocoyo/Gallery/Normal%20Pocoyo.png)

Pocoyo is a 4-year-old boy, who is the main character of Pocoyo. He is the face of the series and has appeared in every episode. He has great drive, he likes to discover the world around him and always lives the world from the perspective of wonder and fascination. His main friends and whom he considers his family are Pato, Elly, Nina, Loula, Sleepy Bird, Baby Bird, Caterpillar, Pulpo, Roberto and Yanko.

Name: Pocoyo

Age: 4 years (Initial), 10 years (Unconfirmed)

Gender: Male

Species: Human

Friends: Pato, Elly, Nina and Loula

First appearance: Umbrella Umbrella

He likes to do: Play, Build, Paint, Explore and Learn
