**Original Title:** *Pocoyó*  
**Genre:** Preschool animation, comedy, interactive 3D exploration  
**Target Audience:** Preschool children (ages 1–6)  
**Running Time:** ~7 minutes per episode  
**Production Companies:** Zinkia Entertainment (Spain), Granada Kids (UK, early seasons)  
**Premiere Date:** January 7, 2005 (Spain)  
**Status:** Ongoing — new content continues on digital platforms  

---

## Overview
**Pocoyo** is a minimalist, 3D-animated preschool series that follows a curious young boy and his friends as they explore, learn, and play.  
The series is known for its clean, white-background world and gentle narration that invites viewers to interact and think along with Pocoyo.

The show’s narrator speaks directly to Pocoyo (and the audience), guiding him through discoveries, emotions, and friendships.  
Its calm tone and simple visual design make it ideal for very young viewers learning to understand the world.

---

## Creation and Production
- Created by **David Cantolla**, **Luis Gallego**, and **Guillermo García Carsí**.  
- Produced by **Zinkia Entertainment** (Spain).  
- Early UK co-productions by **Granada Kids**.  
- The name *“Pocoyo”* comes from a playful Spanish phrase meaning *“little me”* (*poco yo*).  
- Animation created using 3D software (Softimage XSI in early seasons).  
- As of 2025, new content is being developed under **Animaj** and released digitally on YouTube and streaming services.

| **Sleepy Bird** | A relaxed, often-sleeping bird who occasionally joins adventures. |

---

## Themes and Educational Elements
- Encourages **curiosity, friendship, discovery, and empathy**.  
- Uses humor, repetition, and problem-solving appropriate for toddlers.  
- Simple animation style helps young children focus on key actions.  
- Teaches basic concepts like **colors, shapes, counting**, and **emotions**.  

---

## Episodes and Format
- Each episode lasts around **7 minutes**.  
- The series includes **five main seasons** and multiple specials.  
- A **pilot episode** (“The See-Saw”) was produced but not widely released.  
- New episodes and shorts are frequently published on **YouTube** and digital platforms.  

---

## Broadcast and Distribution
- Aired in **over 150 countries** and translated into multiple languages.  
- Originally broadcast in Spain on **TVE** and in the UK on **CITV**.  
- Later distributed on **YouTube**, **Netflix**, and **other streaming platforms**.  
- Maintains a strong online presence through **pocoyo.com** and official social media.  

---

## Awards and Recognition
- **BAFTA Award** for Best Preschool Animation.  
- **Annecy International Animated Film Festival Award** for Animation Excellence.  
- Praised for innovation in visual design and early learning approach.  

---

## Legacy
- Recognized as one of the most iconic preschool series of the 2000s.  
- Known for its **clean animation, educational tone**, and **interactive narration**.  
- Continues to attract audiences through YouTube, apps, and educational spin-offs.  
- Used in language-learning contexts for its slow, clear narration and simple visuals.
